About Us

Founded in 2015, our organization is dedicated to empowering women in the tech industry and promoting gender diversity and equality in the rapidly evolving world of technology. We believe that diversity fuels innovation, and by empowering women, we can unlock the full potential of the tech sector.

Our Why

We recognize the systemic barriers and biases that have historically hindered women's progress in technology and are committed to addressing them head-on.
